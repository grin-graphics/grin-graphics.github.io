About WiJ

WiJ was created to give a basic understanding of jQuery and jQuery plugins to designers and developers alike. The site is intended to give a short summary of the basics of jQuery in order to help push students in the right direction when learning jQuery. WiJ was a 2 week side project made by one person as an assignment to learn about basic jQuery.
